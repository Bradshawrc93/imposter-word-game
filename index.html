🎭 Imposter Word Game

Can you spot the imposters?

📋 How to Play

🎯 Setup

• Choose 3-10 players
• Select 1-5 imposters
• Pick a category or go random
• Player 1 is never an imposter (they manage the device)
• Most players get the secret word, imposters get "IMPOSTER"

🎮 Giving Clues

Each player says 1 word associated with the secret word:
• Cryptic enough that imposters can't figure it out
• Clear enough that other players know you have the real word
• Imposters must bluff based on the category and other clues

🗳️ Voting Phase

After all clues are given:
• Vote for who you think is an imposter
• Majority vote eliminates that player
• Voted-out player reveals if they were Player or Imposter

🎯 Single Imposter

If Imposter voted out: Gets 1 guess at secret word
• Correct guess = Imposter wins
• Wrong guess = Players win

If Player voted out: Second round of clues
• Find imposter = Players win
• Vote out another player = Imposter wins

👥 Multiple Imposters

If Imposter voted out: ALL remaining imposters take turns guessing
• Any correct guess = All Imposters win
• All wrong guesses = Players win

If Player voted out: Continue to next round
• Maximum 2 rounds total
• No elimination after 2 rounds = Imposters win
